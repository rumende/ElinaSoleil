Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Elina Soleil (AI travel assistant) collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Our application does not collect personal data. It interacts with public Instagram content and responds to user messages through automation.

Responses are generated by an AI model (GPT-4) and do not store personal data. No information is shared with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Our automation is powered by n8n and OpenAI API. Instagram Graph API is used to publish and respond to content.

4. Security

All access tokens and keys are stored securely and never exposed publicly.
